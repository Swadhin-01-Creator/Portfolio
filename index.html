01 — About Me

Who I Am

I'm Swadhin Das, a systems engineer with a deep passion for building things that operate at the intersection of hardware, signal processing, and intelligent software. Whether it's architecting digital logic at the gate level or building full-stack applications, I approach every challenge with precision and curiosity.

My work spans wireless communication systems, ASIC/FPGA design, machine learning applications, and software development — driven by a belief that the most impactful systems are those that seamlessly integrate multiple domains of engineering.

When I'm not designing circuits or crunching signal data, I'm exploring new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what's computationally possible.
